ICP Scoring Enrichment Pipeline Nexen Capital

B2B SaaS Lead Scoring & Enrichment Pipeline

Nexen Capital needed a repeatable way to identify which B2B SaaS startups were worth pursuing -- and which weren't. I designed and built a full enrichment and ICP scoring pipeline that pulls raw leads from Apollo, enriches them across three data sources, runs them through a weighted scoring rubric, and writes the results directly to HubSpot as 11 custom properties. The architecture separates batch processing (Clay) from ongoing production automation (Make) to keep costs rational as volume scales.
